Project Title: Car Price Classification

Description:

The Car Price Classification project is a simple Shiny app that allows users to upload a CSV file containing car price data. The app reads the CSV file, classifies the cars based on price range, and displays the distribution using a pie chart.

Features:

- Upload a CSV file containing car price data
- Classify cars based on price range
- Visualize the distribution of car prices using a pie chart

Usage:

1. Upload a CSV file containing car price data.
2. View the distribution of car prices based on price range.

Data:

The app expects the CSV file to have a column named "Selling_Price" containing the selling price of cars.
